# HOW TO USE YOUR SECRET SPY LENS

Follow these steps to solve secret codes:

1. **Open the App:** Tap the "HashItOut Lens" icon on your phone.
2. **Point the Camera:** Point your phone at the secret code. 
3. **Freeze the Screen:** Tap the **FREEZE** button. Now the picture won't move.
4. **Draw a Box:** Use your finger to draw a box around the secret code on the screen.
5. **Wait for the Magic:** You will see a list of results pop up.
6. **Pick the Best One:** Tap the result that looks like real English words.
7. **See the Code Change:** The box on the screen will change to show the real words right on top of the code!
8. **Save It:** Tap the **EXPORT** button to save a picture of your work to show your friends.

**Important Tip:** If it looks like a bunch of symbols (like % or #), it's probably wrong. Pick the one that looks like a real sentence!
